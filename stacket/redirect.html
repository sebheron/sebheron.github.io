Stacket - The to-do board for developers

Stacket is a to-do board application originally developed for personal usage, which aims to provide a platform for solo developers to manage their projects effectively.

Here's a few reasons why we think Stacket works for us and why we think it would work for you:
‍
Simple and User Friendly
Stacket is designed to be used by anyone from Students to Engineers, its simple interface won't keep you guessing and you can plan and organise projects however you like.

Integrates right into Windows
Stacket is designed to work and integrate right into your Windows workspace, it will sit nicely on the top of the screen ready for you to make changes whenever you need.
‍
Completely Offline
Stacket was designed for solo endeavours, so it works out of the box with no accounts needed.

Free!
Stacket is free and available to everyone.

Stacket has numerous features, here are a few of the main ones:

  • Add, remove, edit and move items across the board however you see fit.
  • A toggleable window.
  • As many boards as you need.
  • Colourful boards.
  • A completely customisable workspace which stays the way you like it.
